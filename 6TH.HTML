Event Highlights

Each club will host a series of events, workshops, and competitions throughout the three days of Shruthi 2025. Participants can look forward to:

  • Dance and music performances
  • Art exhibitions
  • Technical workshops and hackathons
  • Social service initiatives
  • Sports tournaments
  • Literary debates and quizzes
