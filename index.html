Sudhee 2024
Sudhee 2024 was a technical festival at CBIT Hyderabad with various events like Civilizations, NEOZION, Cybernexa, and HEADSTART.
  • Civilizations: Paper presentations & water splash events.
  • NEOZION: National-level biotech symposium.
  • Cybernexa: IoT, Cybersecurity, Blockchain focus.
  • Hackathon: Rs. 30,000 prize pool.
Shruthi Day-1 2024
Shruthi 2024 was CBIT’s annual sports and cultural fest from Feb 28 to Mar 1.
  • Sports Day: Feb 29 (4:30–9:30 PM)
  • Institute Day: Mar 1 (5:30–10:00 PM)
  • Dandiya Night: Cultural dance celebration.
Shruthi Day-2 2024
Shruthi Day-2 included sports, cultural performances, and creative poster competitions.
  • Festival celebrated unity and joy.
  • Events shared on Instagram & Facebook.
AURA 2024
AURA 2024 was CBIT’s national-level sports festival from Nov 14–16.
  • Over 10 sports categories (indoor & outdoor).
  • Promoted sportsmanship and healthy competition.
  • Attracted participants from top universities.
