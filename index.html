SUDHEE 2024

VIEW MORE
Sudhee 2024 was a technical festival at Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT) in Hyderabad, India. The festival included a number of events, including Civilizations, NEOZION, Cybernexa, and HEADSTART. Sudhee 2024 events Civilizations A two-day festival held on February 26–27, 2024, that included paper presentations, water splash, and other events. NEOZION A two-day national-level technical symposium that celebrated biotechnological research. Cybernexa A two-day college-level technical festival that focused on IoT, cybersecurity, and blockchain. HEADSTART A two-day national-level technical festival that showcased students' technical skills and achievements. Hackathon A hackathon that offered a prize pool of Rs. 30,000, participation certificates, and networking opportunities. Sudhee is a college-wide technical festival at CBIT that provides a platform for students to showcase their skills and learn about the latest advances in various fields

AURA 2024

VIEW MORE
AURA 2024 was a national-level sports festival held at Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT) in Hyderabad, Telangana from November 14–16, 2024. The event was organized by the Department of Physical Education and the sports club, Chaitanya Kreeda. Features AURA 2024 included more than 10 sports categories, both indoor and outdoor The event aimed to promote sportsmanship, teamwork, and healthy competition It attracted participation from top universities and colleges across India The event included cultural programs The event concluded with a valedictory ceremony on November 16, 2024
